The Best Movies According to Tavo

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Blade Runner: The Director's Cut

This is my favourite movie of all time. The rooftop scene between Harrison Ford's Deckard and Rutger Hauer's Roy Batty is movie's classic.

Dune: Part One

I love Frank Herbert's book. Director's Denis Villeneuve adaption is top-notch work.

La La Land

Great and beautiful film. Really inspired performances by both leads and the film score is awesome.
